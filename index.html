🔔 Deal Alerts

Get notified when LEGO sets hit 50%+ off, or when your specific watched sets go on sale. All configured via Render environment variables.

📱 Push Notifications (Free)

1. Download the free ntfy app (iOS/Android) or visit ntfy.sh
2. In Render → Environment, add:

NTFY_TOPIC = lego-deals-yourname123

3. Subscribe to that topic in the ntfy app
4. Done! Instant phone alerts on deals
Not configured

📧 Email Alerts (Gmail)

1. Enable 2FA on your Google account
2. Go to myaccount.google.com → Security → App Passwords
3. Generate an App Password for "Mail"
4. Add to Render Environment:

ALERT_EMAIL_FROM = your Gmail
ALERT_EMAIL_PASS = 16-char app password
ALERT_EMAIL_TO = destination email
Not configured

🎯 Watched Sets

Add set numbers — you'll get alerted the moment they go on sale anywhere.

Copy the final list to Render as WATCHED_SETS = 75375,42151

⚙️ Settings

Set alert threshold in Render Environment:

ALERT_MIN_DISCOUNT = 50

(Change to 30 for 30%+ off alerts, etc.)
